# Monetisation Plan

## Product Options

### 1. Consumer Game

A cosy AI world simulator for players who enjoy watching stories unfold.

Possible pricing:

- Free demo.
- Early access: GBP 4.99 to GBP 14.99.
- Full version later: GBP 19.99+.

### 2. Creator Tool

Users create AI worlds, characters, and story simulations.

Possible pricing:

- Free basic tier.
- Creator plan around GBP 9.99/month.
- Paid world templates.

### 3. Education Version

Teachers use the simulator for:

- History villages.
- Business teams.
- Social situations.
- Debate practice.
- Leadership scenarios.

Possible pricing:

- GBP 99/year individual teacher.
- School licence later.

### 4. Coaching And Training Simulator

Businesses use it to simulate:

- Customer service.
- Team conflict.
- Sales conversations.
- Leadership decisions.

Possible pricing:

- GBP 29/month solo.
- GBP 99+/month team.

## Best First Market

Start with:

```text
AI world simulator for creators, streamers, and educators.
```

## Why People May Buy It

- It is visual.
- It is fun to watch.
- It creates stories automatically.
- It can be customised.
- It can be educational.
- It can be used for content creation.

## First Sellable Version

A downloadable or hosted demo with:

- Three world templates.
- Twelve character templates.
- Save/load.
- Export story log.
- Custom world event prompts.
