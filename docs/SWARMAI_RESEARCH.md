# SwarmAI Research

Repo: https://github.com/xg-gh-25/SwarmAI.git

## Purpose

Evaluate SwarmAI as a possible multi-agent swarm intelligence layer for NEXUS Worlds.

## Potential Uses

- AI civilisation group decision-making
- Agent teams and factions
- Swarm planning
- Automated world development
- Multi-agent debates
- Collective research and invention
- Planet evolution decisions

## Integration Rule

Do not merge SwarmAI directly into the main app until the licence, stack, dependencies and runtime behaviour are reviewed.

## Possible Integration

NEXUS Worlds should keep its main simulation in Convex and TypeScript. SwarmAI may later run as a separate service or research reference.

## Best Fit

SwarmAI-style logic could help groups of agents decide how to evolve the planet together, such as whether to build farms, research electricity, create flying cars, form factions, expand settlements, or respond to disasters.
